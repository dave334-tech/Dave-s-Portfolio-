About Me

Hi, I’m Paul David Onotu, an aspiring graphic and logo designer with a passion for bringing ideas to life through creative visuals. I love exploring colors, shapes, and typography to craft designs that make a lasting impression. Though I’m still learning and growing in the field, I have a burning desire to master my craft and create works that inspire others.

My Past

My journey into design started with a curiosity for art and creativity. I’ve experimented with tools like Canva and Photoshop to create my first few pieces, from simple posters to brands. Each project taught me something new about design and deepened my love for visual storytelling.

My Present

Currently, I am refining my skills in graphic design. I am exploring advanced tools, studying design trends, and working on practice projects to build a strong portfolio. My goal is to combine creativity with functionality in every design I create. I’m actively practicing with tools like Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Canva, and exploring platforms like Figma to better understand layout and interface design. I follow top design influencers and communities to stay updated on current trends, color palettes, and branding techniques. To challenge myself, I regularly take on personal projects and design prompts. Whether it's redesigning a famous logo, creating concept posters, or experimenting with typography, these projects help me apply what I learn and push my creativity further.

My Future ambition

Looking ahead, I see myself as a professional designer working with brands and individuals to create powerful visual identities. I plan to expand my skill set, work on real client projects, and contribute to the creative community. I plan to immerse myself in real-world projects, building a diverse portfolio that showcases my ability to adapt, innovate, and solve design challenges creatively. As I gain experience, I aim to develop my own unique design style—one that blends simplicity with boldness and functionality with aesthetics.
